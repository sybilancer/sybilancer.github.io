Welcome mat

I'm Noelle or Sybil of Ewa, and this is my website!!

It's intended as a digital collage or journal for all my half-finished projects. Have fun browsing, and please sign the guest book (when i make it). :>

About

I'm a full time mining engineering student and part time lifeguard juggling a million hobbies. I like TTRPGS, geology and making art in an assortment of physical mediums. I'm currently trying to read and get outside more - goals which often conflict.

Vision

On the pegboard adjacent to my desk I have a clip which holds countless sheets of notepaper, flyers, posters and photos that I meant to get to 'later'. This 'later', of course, very rarely comes and these scribbles and brainstorms and little notes to myself lose their meaning by the time I shuffle through them looking for more space for new loose ideas. Then they get thrown away. It's a bit tragic.

This website is hopefully going to be a place for me to get ramblings and ideas out of my brain in a semi-complete form. I don't want things to be polished to a shine or perfect, but I'd like them to legible and complete enough that I can always pick up on them again. It feels like a good thesis for the project. Beyond that, this is a way for me to get a little more computer literate and spend some time with a close friend. You know who you are.
